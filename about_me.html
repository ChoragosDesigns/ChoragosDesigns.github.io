About Me

My name is Nickolas Santiago and I am front-end web developer curently located in the Bronx, New York. I am a soon-to-be-graduate from the Rochester Institute of Technology where I studied New Media Interactive Development.

I am profficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have experience with a number of JavaScript libraries including jQuery, React, d3, and Angular. On the technical side, I also have some experience working with PHP and MySQL. On the design side, I have worked with PhotoShop and Illustrator.

In the past, I have learned to create websites from the ground-up and create interactive web experiences from beginning concept stages to completion. I also have worked on large group projects like the Inspire project and Fragile Equilibrium. I can confidently take design comps and translate them into functional code.

Aside from my technical skills, I am a fast learner, very adaptive, and have a strong work ethic.
